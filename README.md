# WordBopper

WordBopper is a fast, bouncy word game for iPhone and iPad. Tap letter bubbles, make words, score points, and try to beat your best round before the clock runs out.

## How It Works

Each round gives you a 5 by 5 grid of letter bubbles and 2 minutes on the clock. Tap letters to build a word, then use Make Word to score it.

Connected words earn bonus points. Make enough connected words in a row and you unlock a short 3 times scoring boost.

## Features

- Quick 2-minute rounds
- Letter-bopping word play
- Connected-word bonuses
- 3 times scoring boost
- Best-game tracking for score, longest word, word count, and largest chain
- Original game sounds

## Accessibility

WordBopper is built with VoiceOver play in mind. The game uses clear screen headings, large touch targets, spoken gameplay feedback, and an audio-forward design so the experience is playable without relying on sight.

Accessibility is part of the core game design, not an add-on.

## Privacy

WordBopper does not collect personal information, use analytics, show ads, track users, or send gameplay data anywhere.

Best-game statistics are stored locally on your device.
