How to play

  • Tap letter bubbles anywhere on the 5x5 grid to build words.
  • If you build a word with connected letters, you get a bonus.
  • Make three connected words in a row to activate a timed 3x bonus.
  • Hit make word to score.
  • Used letters are replaced instantly.
  • Hint: Explore by Touch works the best on mobile!
  • 2 minutes on the clock. Go!
